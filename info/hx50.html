Agappe Mispa HX 50

5 Part Hematology Analyzer – 60 Tests/Hr

Mispa HX50 is the 5 Part Hematology Analyser with 3rd Generation Technology using Nucleic Acid Fluorescence Staining + Tri Angle laser scattering with throughput of 60 T/Hr. Unlike traditional chemistry staining reagents, our specialized fluorescent staining solution targets DNA or RNA, allowing blind dyeing that corresponds to different cell concentrations of nucleic acid. This results in a depth of dying proportional to the DNA/RNA levels - the stronger the nucleic acid presence, the more robust the fluorescent signal. Leveraging this specificity towards nucleic acid, our 3rd Generation technology is exceptionally sensitive in differentiating leukocytes, especially abnormal cells, making it an invaluable asset in clinical diagnostics.
