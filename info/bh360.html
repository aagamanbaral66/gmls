Bioline BH 360

Automatic 3-Diff Hematology Analyzer

Bioline BH 360 is an automatic 3-part differential hematology analyzer designed for fast, accurate, and reliable CBC testing. With 60 samples/hour throughput, 22 parameters, and 3 histograms, it offers consistent performance with low maintenance. Compact and user-friendly, it's ideal for routine labs, satellite labs, and emergency care units, delivering quality results at a cost-effective price.
